Ballyshaners Parade 2026

The Alexandria St. Patrick’s Day Parade returns for its 43rd year, bringing Irish culture to Old Town. Beyond the celebration, the event highlights Alexandria’s long-standing tradition and its impact on local businesses and community spirit.

HumanitiesDC Community Showcase

The HumanitiesDC Community Showcase highlighted 13 grant-funded projects in DC, where local organizations presented programs and projects ranging from documentaries and photography to poetry and historical tours. Each presentation focused on preserving and sharing the city’s diverse stories, giving a platform to voices and the history of the various communities in the District.
